# Day 14 – AI Job Red Flag Detector

## Objective
Analyze a technical job description and company information using Claude AI to identify hiring risks, workplace red flags, and positive signals before applying.

## What I Did
- Uploaded the Technical JD and Company Information PDF.
- Used Claude AI to analyze the job opportunity.
- Reviewed the Overall Risk Score.
- Identified major hiring red flags.
- Reviewed positive signals about the company.
- Generated smart interview questions.
- Understood the final hiring recommendation.

## Overall Risk Score
82/100 (High Risk)

## Top Red Flags
- Junior role asking for 5+ years of experience.
- Extremely broad technology stack.
- 24/7 support and hustle culture.
- Remote role may require relocation.
- Salary information not disclosed.

## Positive Signals
- Opportunity to work on AI/ML projects.
- High ownership and learning opportunities.
- Direct interaction with leadership.

## Final Verdict
Avoid this opportunity unless the company provides clear answers regarding salary, remote policy, and work culture.

## Key Learnings
- Always analyze a Job Description before applying.
- Hidden red flags can be identified using AI.
- Asking the right interview questions helps avoid risky job offers.
- AI can support smarter career decisions.
