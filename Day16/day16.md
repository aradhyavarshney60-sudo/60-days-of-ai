# Day 16 – Build Your First Stock Research Skill

## Objective

Today's goal was to build a reusable Claude Custom Skill for stock fundamental research instead of repeatedly writing long prompts. The objective was to create a structured workflow that generates consistent, evidence-based stock analysis.

---

## Custom Skill

**Skill Name:** `stock-fundamental-research`

### Description

A reusable Claude Custom Skill designed to analyze Indian listed companies using fundamental analysis. The skill generates structured reports including valuation, profitability, financial health, business quality, strengths, risks, and price trends while avoiding investment recommendations.

---

## Company Analyzed

**Reliance Industries Ltd. (NSE: RELIANCE)**

### Analysis Summary

Using the custom skill, I generated a Quick Take report for Reliance Industries covering:

- Current Market Price (CMP)
- Market Capitalization
- Valuation (P/E Analysis)
- Revenue & Profit Growth
- ROE and ROCE
- Debt Position
- Business Strengths
- Watch Points & Risks
- Fundamental Quality Assessment
- Stock Price Performance Charts

---

## Key Learnings

- Learned how Claude Custom Skills eliminate repetitive prompting.
- Built a reusable workflow for stock research.
- Understood how AI can organize financial data into structured reports.
- Explored valuation metrics, profitability ratios, and business quality.
- Reinforced the importance of verifying financial information before making investment decisions.
