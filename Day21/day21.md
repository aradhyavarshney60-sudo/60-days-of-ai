# Day 21 – Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard using Claude AI that analyzes a user's digital footprint, estimates privacy exposure, and generates an interactive HTML dashboard.

---

## What I Built

Today I created an interactive Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard that visualizes a user's online footprint and privacy exposure.

The dashboard includes:

- Digital Footprint Score
- Privacy Score
- Exposure Heatmap
- Company Exposure Ranking
- Data Collection Matrix
- Risk Radar
- Digital Twin Profile
- Most Valuable Data Assets
- Privacy Improvement Plan
- Final Privacy Verdict

The project was generated with Claude AI and exported as a complete HTML dashboard.
