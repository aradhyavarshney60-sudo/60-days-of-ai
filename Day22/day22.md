# Day 22 – Validate Your Startup Idea Like a VC

## Challenge
Used Claude AI to validate a startup idea and generate a VC-style startup validation report.

## Startup Idea
AI Resume & Interview Coach

## Problem Statement
Many students struggle to create ATS-friendly resumes and prepare effectively for interviews.

## Target Audience
- College Students
- Fresh Graduates
- Job Seekers
- Early Career Professionals

## Deliverables
- Startup Validation Report (PDF)
- Startup Validation Report (DOCX)
- Report Screenshots

## Key Insights
- Founder-Market Fit Analysis
- TAM, SAM & SOM Estimation
- Competitor Analysis
- Customer Persona
- Risk Assessment
- Go / No-Go Recommendation
- 30-Day Action Plan

## Key Learnings
- Validate the problem before building the product.
- Understand customer pain points.
- Analyze competitors before launching.
- AI can speed up startup research and planning.

## Tools Used
- Claude AI
- GitHub
- Markdown

## Outcome
Successfully created a VC-style startup validation report for an AI Resume & Interview Coach and learned how founders validate startup ideas before product development.
