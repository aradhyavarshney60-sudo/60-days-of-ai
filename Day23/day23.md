# Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint

## Objective

Build a Customer & MVP Blueprint for the AI Resume & Interview Coach startup using the Startup Validation Report.

---

## What I Learned

- Identified the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Created a detailed Buyer Persona
- Listed the Top 10 Customer Pain Points
- Designed the complete Customer Journey
- Identified Key Buying Triggers and Customer Objections
- Prioritized features using the MoSCoW Framework
- Defined the MVP scope
- Created a Pricing Hypothesis
- Analyzed Top 5 Startup Risks
- Prepared a 30-Day MVP Execution Plan
- Built a Founder Action Sheet
- Evaluated Customer Clarity, PMF Potential and MVP Readiness

---

## Deliverables

- Customer_MVP_Blueprint_AI_Resume_Coach.pdf

---

## Key Insights

- Focus on one niche customer segment before expanding.
- Validate willingness to pay before building advanced features.
- Keep the MVP simple and solve one core problem exceptionally well.
- Real customer interviews are more valuable than assumptions.
- Early feedback is the strongest indicator of product-market fit.
