# Day 25 – Build an AI Shark Tank Simulator

## Objective
Build an AI-powered Shark Tank Simulator that allows users to pitch their startup ideas, answer investor questions, receive AI-based evaluations, and generate a professional pitch report.

## What I Built
- AI Shark Tank Simulator (Single HTML File)
- Interactive startup pitch experience
- AI investor question system
- Startup evaluation scorecard
- Investment decision engine
- Automated Pitch Report (PDF)

## Project Files
- shark-tank-simulator.html
- SkillSync AI — Pitch Report.pdf

## Features
- Startup idea input form
- Four AI investor personas
- Interactive Q&A session
- AI-powered startup evaluation
- Investment scorecard
- Suggested valuation
- Acquisition/Funding decision
- Downloadable pitch report

## Key Learnings
- Built an interactive business simulation using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Learned how AI can simulate startup investor interviews.
- Understood startup evaluation metrics used by investors.
- Explored valuation, funding, and investment decision concepts.
- Improved frontend development and application design skills.

## Outcome
Successfully developed an AI Shark Tank Simulator that evaluates startup ideas, generates investment decisions, and produces a professional pitch report.
