# Day 29: Operation Lifeline – Supply Chain Crisis Simulation

## 📌 Overview

On Day 29 of my **60 Days AI Challenge**, I completed **Operation Lifeline**, an interactive supply chain crisis simulation. The simulation placed me in the role of a CEO managing a cyberattack that disrupted global logistics, requiring strategic decisions across crisis response, supplier negotiations, executive leadership, and AI investments.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Respond to a supply chain disruption in real time.
- Balance operational continuity with business costs.
- Negotiate effectively with suppliers.
- Lead executive-level crisis decisions.
- Build long-term resilience using AI.

## 🛠️ Decisions Made

### Crisis Response
- Activated a backup supplier.
- Reallocated inventory to affected regions.
- Maintained proactive customer communication.

### Supplier Negotiation
- Negotiated a phased price increase.
- Chose a 6-month trial commitment.
- Offered shared-risk co-investment.
- Preferred strategic partnership over supplier exclusivity.

### Executive Leadership
- Communicated transparently with investors.
- Personally engaged key customers.
- Protected customer-facing operations during cost reductions.
- Conducted company-wide employee updates.
- Proposed supplier diversification for long-term resilience.

### AI Investments
- Demand Forecasting
- Supplier Risk Monitoring
