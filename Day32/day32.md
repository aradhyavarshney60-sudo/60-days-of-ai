# Day 32 – Think Like a Marketing Strategist

## Challenge
Created an interactive Marketing Strategy Simulator using Claude that teaches audience-first marketing through practical business scenarios.

## What I Built
- Single-file HTML application
- Interactive marketing strategy simulator
- Audience analysis module
- Platform selection workflow
- Content pillar planning
- 30-day marketing roadmap
- Marketing crisis response simulation
- Final growth report dashboard

## Scenario
Business: Kids' STEM Toy Startup

Target Audience:
Parents of children aged 6–12

Selected Platforms:
- Instagram
- YouTube
- Email Newsletter

Content Pillars:
- Product Education
- Customer Stories
- Behind the Scenes

## Key Learnings
- Great marketing starts with understanding the audience.
- Choosing the right platforms is more effective than being present everywhere.
- Consistent content pillars strengthen brand identity.
- Long-term trust is built through transparent communication during challenges.
- A structured marketing roadmap improves execution and growth.
