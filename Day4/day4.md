# Day 4 – Chain-of-Thought Prompting

## Objective
Learn how Chain-of-Thought prompting improves AI responses by reasoning step by step.

## My Inputs
- Current Situation: Student
- Skills: Python/Coding, Data Analysis
- Target Goal: Become an AI/ML Engineer
- Timeline: 6 Months

## Key Learnings
- Breaking a problem into steps improves response quality.
- AI provides more personalized recommendations when enough context is given.
- Structured prompting leads to better career planning.

## Biggest Insight
Chain-of-Thought prompting creates a roadmap that is more detailed and personalized than a normal prompt.
